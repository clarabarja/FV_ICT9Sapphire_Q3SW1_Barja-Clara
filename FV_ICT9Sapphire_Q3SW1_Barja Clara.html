Before Sunrise

Directed by Richard Linklater





Meeting by chance on a train and supposedly getting off at different places, Jesse (Ehtan Hawke) and Celine (Julie Delpy) meet. After talking and realizing this strong connection between them, they decide to get off the train in Vienna and wander the city together.



Spending the whole day and night, sharing stories about their lives and thoughts, their bond grows. Just before Celine leaves for Paris, they find that they haven fallen for each other. Will they meet again, or will they just leave this trip in their memories?




Jesse and Celine




Celine


Celine is from Paris, France, a college student at the Sorbonne. A very beautiful and intelligent woman, she's full of idealisms, especially about society and human connection, and love for the world around her. She wants to be the perfect example of an independent and powerful woman, while also striving to find true love. She meets Jesse while on her train back from Budapest returning to Paris and immediately falls in love, delaying her return home to spend the day with him.




Jesse Wallace


Jesse Wallace is a free-spirited and spontaneous American travelling back to the United States of America after his failed visit to his ex-girlfriend in Madrid. He decides to take the train and wander Europe for a while to clear his head when he meets Celine, and he invites her to join him in Vienna, finding himself very interested in her. Currently unemployed but aspiring to be a writer, Jesse is smart, witty, and romantic with many thoughtful insights about life. He aims to find peace and joy in his life, living the way he feels will make him happiest.
